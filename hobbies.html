Journaling

Journaling can mean many things such as writing in a diary, writing therapy, journal therapy, journaling file system, or electronic message journaling but the most common and popular form is the use of a diary. Diaries are a written record, with entries serperated by dates representing events that have occured in the time frame mentioned.

How I use Journaling

I journal somewhat often now, but I used to journal every single day back then with each entry lasting about a page and a half. When I first began, they only took up about half a page or even a quarter, but now they take up more than a page and a half. I would always write down anything I noticed that I had seen in the day, some of my thoughts, and even some struggles I've been in.

Walking

Walking is a form of locomotion for all land creatures such as humans, animals, bugs, and all land lifeforms. Walking can also be the act of going for a stride for a breather, to get some exercise, to view your surrounding scenery, hanging out with friends, or any reason that suits you.

How I Use Walking

Although I don't walk often, when I'm able to, I always enjoy it. Walking is one of my favorite activites to do when I leave my home because I usually walk in forests, or parks and I'm able to enjoy the scenery. The sky I see of the sunset, the clouds moving around changing color, and the dark sky that comes after. Or even in the day, the light that shines all over the land is beautiful and brings life to everything around me.
